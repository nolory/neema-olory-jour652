Expected lifetime drastically impacted by End Stage Renal Disease

STORY BY: NEEMA OLORY

End Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) affects many Americans and can drastically impact expected lifetime years even with modern day advances in the medical field. Over half a million Americans live with ESRD. More men than women have ESRD. The largest age group with ESRD are those between the ages of 60 - 69 years.
